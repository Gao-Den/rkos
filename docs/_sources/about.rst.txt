About
========================================

.. image:: _static/rk-logo-social-color-non-bg.png
   :width: 20%
   :alt: RKOS Logo

RK is a preemptive RTOS designed to bridge the gap between operating system theory and real-world application. By providing a clean, accessible architecture, it strips away the complexity of traditional kernels to inspire and empower the embedded systems community. 

Whether you are learning or building, RK is your transparent blueprint for efficient.
